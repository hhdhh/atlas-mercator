## Cut-off Times
Orders placed before **15:00 local origin time** (Shenzhen) ship same day.
Weekend orders ship on Monday.

## Tracking
- All orders receive a tracking link via email within 24h of dispatch.
- For untracked standard shipments, allow 5 business days before raising a
  "where is my order" ticket.
