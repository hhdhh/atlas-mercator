# Warranty Terms (保修政策)

## Electronics — 12 months
- Covers: battery degradation (>30% in 12 months), driver / sensor failure,
  charging circuit defects, water ingress under IP-rating use.
- Does not cover: cosmetic damage, third-party modifications, accidents.

## Wearables — 12 months
- Watch bands excluded after 30 days.
- Battery covered for 6 months only (consumable part).

## Home Appliances — 24 months
- Includes free parts + labor for EU customers (statutory minimum).

## Filing a claim
1. Submit ticket with order ID + short video / photos of issue.
2. Support team triages within 24h business hours.
3. Resolution: replacement / repair / refund based on stock and defect category.
