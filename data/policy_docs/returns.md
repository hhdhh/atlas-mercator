# Returns & Refunds Policy (政策)

## 30-day No-Reason Return
- Applies to all unopened, resellable items within **30 days** of delivery.
- Buyer pays return shipping via local carrier of choice.
- Refund issued within **5 business days** of warehouse receipt.

## 60-day Quality-Related Return
- Covers DOA (dead-on-arrival) and verified manufacturing defects.
- Return shipping **prepaid** by Atlas Mercator (DHL label provided).
- Replacement shipped immediately upon warehouse receipt; refund issued if stock-out.

## Exclusions
- Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page.
- Used electronics with physical damage not caused by manufacturing.
- Gift cards and digital products.
